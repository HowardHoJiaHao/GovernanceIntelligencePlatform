=== CORPORATE STRATEGY AND ESG FRAMEWORK ===

Section 1.1: Corporate Vision

The organisation aims to become a regional leader in sustainable digital transformation through innovation, governance excellence, and responsible investment practices.

Section 1.2: Strategic Objectives (2026 - 2030)

Objective 1:
Accelerate digitalisation across all business units.

Target:
- 80% of business processes digitised by 2030.
- 100% paperless procurement process by 2028.

Objective 2:
Strengthen data-driven decision making.

Target:
- All executive reports must utilise data analytics and AI-assisted insights.
- Enterprise-wide knowledge management platform implementation by 2027.

Objective 3:
Improve operational efficiency.

Target:
- Reduce manual administrative effort by 30%.
- Improve document retrieval efficiency by 50%.

Section 1.3: ESG Commitments

Environmental

- Reduce organisational carbon emissions by 25% by 2030.
- All major suppliers must provide sustainability declarations.
- New projects must consider environmental impact assessments.

Social

- Minimum 40 hours of employee training annually.
- Equal opportunity hiring policy.
- Community investment initiatives in education and technology.

Governance

- Zero tolerance for fraud and corruption.
- Annual governance audits are mandatory.
- Procurement decisions must remain transparent and auditable.

Section 1.4: Supplier Sustainability Requirements

Suppliers participating in projects above RM 500,000 must:

- Comply with environmental regulations.
- Submit sustainability declarations.
- Demonstrate ethical business practices.

Section 1.5: Digital Transformation Priorities

Priority 1:
AI-enabled knowledge management.

Priority 2:
Enterprise data governance.

Priority 3:
Intelligent procurement analytics.

Priority 4:
Cybersecurity enhancement.
