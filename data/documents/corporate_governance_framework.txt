=== CORPORATE GOVERNANCE FRAMEWORK ===

Section 1.1: Authority Matrix

Project Managers
- Approval authority up to RM 50,000.
- Any expenditure exceeding RM 50,000 requires escalation to the Department Head.

Department Heads
- Approval authority up to RM 250,000.
- Capital expenditure requests exceeding RM 250,000 require Divisional Managing Director approval.

Divisional Managing Directors
- Approval authority up to RM 500,000 for operational expenditure.
- Approval authority up to RM 2,000,000 for strategic initiatives.

Board of Directors
- Mandatory approval for all capital expenditure exceeding RM 5,000,000.
- Mandatory approval for mergers, acquisitions, and strategic investments.

Section 1.2: Governance Principles

- Transparency in decision-making.
- Accountability at all management levels.
- Proper segregation of duties.
- Compliance with regulatory requirements.

Section 1.3: Document Retention

- Governance records must be retained for seven years.
- Board meeting minutes must be retained indefinitely.
