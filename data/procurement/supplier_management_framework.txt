=== SUPPLIER MANAGEMENT FRAMEWORK ===

Section 5.1: Supplier Evaluation Weightage

Price = 40%
Technical Capability = 30%
Delivery Timeline = 20%
Support Quality = 10%

Section 5.2: Mandatory Requirements

- Minimum 5 years experience
- ISO 27001 certification
- Minimum 3 enterprise references
- Compliance score above 80%

Section 5.3: High Risk Suppliers

A supplier is classified as High Risk if:

- Compliance score below 70%
- Ongoing legal dispute
- Failed audit findings
- More than 3 late deliveries within 12 months
