=== RISK AND COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK ===

Section 2.1: Risk Categories

Operational Risk

- Process failures
- System downtime
- Human error

Financial Risk

- Budget overruns
- Fraud
- Revenue loss

Compliance Risk

- Regulatory breaches
- Policy violations
- Contractual non-compliance

Cybersecurity Risk

- Data breaches
- Unauthorized access
- Malware incidents

Section 2.2: Risk Classification

Low Risk

- Financial impact below RM 50,000
- No regulatory consequences

Medium Risk

- Financial impact between RM 50,000 and RM 500,000
- Limited operational disruption

High Risk

- Financial impact exceeding RM 500,000
- Reputational damage
- Legal consequences

Critical Risk

- Significant regulatory violation
- Major data breach
- Fraud involving senior management

Section 2.3: Escalation Matrix

Low Risk

- Managed by Department Manager

Medium Risk

- Requires mitigation plan
- Requires Head of Department approval

High Risk

- Immediate escalation to Risk Management Committee

Critical Risk

- Immediate notification to Executive Committee
- Board notification required within 24 hours

Section 2.4: Compliance Requirements

Mandatory Compliance Areas

- Procurement
- Data Privacy
- Financial Reporting
- Anti-Bribery and Anti-Corruption

Section 2.5: Non-Compliance Penalties

Minor Violation

- Written warning

Major Violation

- Internal investigation

Critical Violation

- Suspension of operations
- Regulatory reporting

Section 2.6: Vendor Compliance Requirements

All suppliers must:

- Maintain compliance score above 80%
- Pass annual compliance review
- Report any legal investigations

Suppliers with compliance score below 70% are classified as High Risk.
