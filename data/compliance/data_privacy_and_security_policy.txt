=== DATA PRIVACY AND SECURITY POLICY ===

Section 3.1: Data Classification11111111111111111111111111111111111

Public Data

Definition:
Information approved for public disclosure.

Examples:
- Published annual reports
- Press releases

Internal Data

Definition:
Information intended for employees only.

Examples:
- Internal procedures
- Operational guidelines

Confidential Data

Definition:
Sensitive information requiring restricted access.

Examples:
- Financial records
- Customer information
- Employee records

Restricted Data

Definition:
Highly sensitive information requiring executive approval.

Examples:
- Acquisition plans
- Strategic investment documents
- Board papers

Section 3.2: Access Control Requirements

Employees may only access information necessary for their role.

Access Requests

- Manager approval required.
- Information owner approval required.

Quarterly access reviews are mandatory.

Section 3.3: Password Policy

- Minimum 12 characters.
- Multi-factor authentication mandatory.
- Password expiration every 90 days.

Section 3.4: Data Breach Response

Severity Level 1

- Internal reporting within 24 hours.

Severity Level 2

- Security team notification within 4 hours.

Severity Level 3

- Executive notification within 1 hour.
- Incident response team activation.

Section 3.5: Third-Party Data Handling

Suppliers accessing company systems must:

- Sign confidentiality agreements.
- Pass security assessment.
- Comply with data protection requirements.

Section 3.6: Security Violations

Examples:

- Sharing confidential information
- Unauthorized system access
- Improper data storage

All confirmed security violations are automatically classified as High Risk incidents.

Section 3.7: AI Governance

AI systems must:

- Log all user interactions.
- Maintain audit trails.
- Protect confidential information.
- Restrict access based on user roles.

Sensitive company data must not be exposed to unauthorized users. Edited
